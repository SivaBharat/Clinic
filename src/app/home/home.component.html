Our Services

Short-term hospitalization refers to a relatively brief stay in a hospital, typically for a specific medical condition or treatment that can be addressed within a limited timeframe. This type of hospitalization is usually shorter in duration compared to long-term or extended hospital stays, which may involve extended care.

Prescription services refer to the processes and services involved in obtaining, managing, and fulfilling medical prescriptions for medications and other healthcare products. These services play a crucial role in ensuring that patients receive the right medications as prescribed by their healthcare providers.

Genetic testing is a medical procedure that involves analyzing an individual's DNA to identify specific changes or variations in their genes, chromosomes, or proteins. These changes can be associated with various medical conditions, including inherited diseases, susceptibility to certain illnesses, or even pharmacogenetic information.
